Future Outlook
The Electric Vehicle Transition

By 2025, the delivery sector is projected to drive 25% of all EV sales in India. The economics are compelling — but infrastructure gaps remain.

⚡ Electric Vehicles — Advantages
30–35% cheaper total cost of ownership over vehicle lifetime
~₹0.03/mile operating cost vs. petrol equivalents
Only 3 moving parts vs. 113 in ICE — far lower maintenance
Zero tailpipe emissions in cities where riders breathe most
Battery swap networks enable full-shift productivity
⛽ ICE Vehicles — Persistent Challenges
₹1,500–3,000/month in servicing costs (dust, heat, rain)
₹4,500+ for minor pothole damage, no platform coverage
10–30 day insurance claim assessment = unaffordable downtime
10–15% annual depreciation compounds financial vulnerability
Range anxiety and EV charging gaps still deter adoption

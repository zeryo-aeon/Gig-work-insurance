Weekly Pricing Models

4 Insurance Frameworks

Click any model to see detailed breakdown and trigger conditions.

Model 1

Baseline + Event Guarantee

Minimum weekly income (₹2,500) if active hours met. Disruption hours count as worked.

Trigger: Any verified disruption event
Outcome: Income stability without forcing risky work
Example: Worker logged 40hrs but city flooded 2 days → full ₹2,500 guaranteed
Model 2

Micro-Insurance (5% Pool)

5% of weekly earnings → Resilience Pool (+ platform match). Automatic parametric payout.

Trigger: Weather/event threshold crossed
Outcome: Self-sustained parametric insurance fund
Example: ₹5,000 weekly → ₹250 pooled + ₹250 platform match = ₹500 cover/week
Model 3

Hazard Multiplier Pay

1.5× base pay during Hazard Days when heat/rain thresholds are crossed.

Trigger: Heat index > 42°C or rainfall > 50mm/hr
Outcome: Compensates risk instead of relying on surge pricing
Example: Normal ₹30/delivery → ₹45/delivery on hazard days
Model 4

Tiered Stability Contracts

Fixed weekly schedule → guaranteed income floor. Curfew/lockdown → 70% payout.

Trigger: Curfew / lockdown declaration
Outcome: Predictable weekly earnings
Example: ₹6,000/week contract → ₹4,200 guaranteed during lockdown
